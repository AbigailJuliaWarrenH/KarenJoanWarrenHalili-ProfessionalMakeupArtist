Biography

Karen Joan Warren Halili is a Filipino Profesional Makeup Artist .

She graduated Bachelor of Arts in Production Design at De La Salle - College of Saint Benilde in 2016. After her degree in Production Design was conferred, she began working in one of the Philippinesâ€™ leading media and entertainment organization as a Production Assistant. During her full swing in her profession, she realized that Production Design was somehow a shallow career path for her to embrace her passion and aspiration in life.

In year 2017, she visited San Francisco for a holiday while she was contemplating on her career path. When she came back to Manila, she realized that her passion is in the Makeup Industry. In June 2017, she enrolled for a Certificate in Makeup Course at Tony Galvez School of Cosmetology in Quezon City, Philippines. She graduated November of the same year, 2017.

Karen started her internship under the supervision of two Professional Makeup Artists namely: Ms. Cath Libunao; and Ms. Coney Veto. Both are Professional Makeup Artists and handling hair and makeup team for events in the Philippines. Their specializations are Bridal & Entourage Hair and Makeup.

After her completion of several months of intership, she started her own Freelance Makeup Profession. She has accomodated clients in Manila, Quezon City, Taguig, Makati and nearby provinces such as Tagaytay. Most her clients' events are Weddings, Company Parties, Corporate Ceremonies and Family Celebration.

Every now and then, she visits the United States of America. She has catered with clients in the Filipino Community in Los Angeles & San Francisco, California.

Karen's keen and passionate outlook in this industry made her bring up to date in the world of makeup, fashion, and events.
